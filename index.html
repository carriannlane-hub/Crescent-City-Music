About Crescent City Music

Located in the vibrant heart of New Orleans, Crescent City Music is your premier destination for professional recording services and music education. We combine the rich musical heritage of our city with modern technology and techniques.

Our studio provides a creative environment where artists can explore their sound and bring their musical dreams to reality. From jazz to blues, funk to contemporary, we celebrate all genres while honoring the soulful spirit of New Orleans music.

Under the guidance of owner Claire Duperie, we've built a reputation for excellence in both recording production and music instruction, helping countless artists and students achieve their musical goals.
